ABOUT US

About Us

We are a team of experienced Civil/Structural Design Professionals with backgrounds in different industries such as telecommunication, transportation(bridge & railway),process (oil&gas, refineries and mining)power generation, water infrastructure and offshore engineering.

Our Mission

To provide excellent structural engineering and design solutions in different simple and complex industries with primary consideration on the effectiveness and efficiency of services rendered.
To train internationally competent civil/structural engineers and technologist and contribute to nation’s country-wide development.

Our Vision

To be one of the pioneer in integrating Building Information Modeling (BIM) technology in the engineering, design and construction of buildings and non-building facilities in the Philippines.
To perform outstanding cross-border design practice with Design Professionals in the ASEAN region as well as the APEC communities.

Our Values

Our passion and respect for our beloved profession of civil engineering with emphasis in structural engineering specialization. Being accountable for whatever we do and treating each project’s success as our own team’s success.
